Matrimonial and Family Disputes

Divorce, maintenance, child custody, domestic violence, dowry matters, settlements, and family court proceedings.

Matrimonial and family disputes require not only legal skill but also sensitivity, discretion and practical guidance. At CourtWise, Advocates and Legal Consultants, Lucknow, we assist clients in divorce cases, mutual consent divorce, maintenance proceedings, child custody matters, domestic violence cases, dowry related allegations, restitution of conjugal rights, settlement negotiations, family court proceedings, and connected civil or criminal remedies.

We assist both husbands and wives, as well as families, in understanding their rights, obligations, available remedies, and possible settlement options. Our work includes drafting petitions, replies, affidavits, settlement terms, applications, legal notices, complaints, and representation before Family Courts, Magistrate Courts, Sessions Courts, High Court, and other competent forums.

Whether the client is seeking divorce, defending a matrimonial case, pursuing maintenance, resolving custody issues, responding to allegations, or exploring an amicable settlement, CourtWise provides structured legal support with care, confidentiality and a balanced approach.

For legal advice and representation in Lucknow, Uttar Pradesh, Allahabad High Court, Lucknow Bench, other High Courts or Supreme Court matters, you may contact CourtWise, Advocates and Legal Consultants, for a careful assessment of your case and appropriate legal remedy.
